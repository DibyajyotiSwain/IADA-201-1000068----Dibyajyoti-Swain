MindPatch is a user-centered, AI-assisted digital wellness application prototype designed to help students and young adults manage screen time, reduce anxiety, and build healthy daily habits. ## üìå Project Purpose

In a highly connected digital world, students often experience screen fatigue, emotional stress, and burnout. MindPatch addresses this issue by combining:

- Mood awareness  
- Screen-time reflection

The goal is to **support balance, not enforce restriction**.

### Main Research Findings

Secondary research highlighted that excessive screen time is strongly linked to increased stress, anxiety, and reduced emotional well-being among students. Studies emphasize that **self-awareness, gentle nudging, and habit-based interventions** are more effective than restrictive controls. Research from the **World Health Organization** and peer-reviewed mental health studies informed the app‚Äôs calm design, mood tracking, and ethical AI-based detox suggestions.

We conducted secondary research by analyzing **Headspace** and **Forest** to understand user engagement, habit formation, and emotional design. Insights from their calm UI, motivation techniques, and behavior nudges informed the design, AI logic, and wellness-focused features of MindPatch.



## üß† Key Features

### 1. Mood Check-In  
Users log how they feel using emoji-based sliders. Mood check-ins occur periodically (every 12 hours) to build emotional awareness and personalize suggestions.

### 2. Screen-Time Dashboard  
Users manually input daily screen time.  
Optional permission-based access to screen-time data is **conceptual and simulated**, ensuring ethical and privacy-safe design.

### 3. AI Detox Planner  
The AI planner generates wellness suggestions based on:
- Mood level  
- Screen time  
- Time of day  
- User patterns  

**Example suggestions include:**
- Tech breaks  
- Breathing exercises  
- Hobby focus  
- Light motivational or entertainment content  

_No real AI model is built; logic is simulated using flow-based rules._

### 4. Gratitude Journal  
A calm journaling space for users to write daily positives, encouraging reflection and emotional grounding.

### 5. Habit Tracker & Rewards  
Users follow anxiety-reduction and maintenance habits such as:
- Mindfulness or breathing exercises  
- Reduced screen time before sleep  
- Physical activity  
- Gratitude journaling  

Progress is shown as a **monthly completion path**.  
üéÅ **100% completion unlocks a simulated 12-month gym membership redeem code** (e.g., fitness apps like Cult-style platforms).

### 6. Personal Goals (Private)  
Users can set personal goals that remain private, supporting intrinsic motivation without social pressure.
